A Window of Discourse

Wednesday Jan 9, 2019

The Overton Toolkit is a library of open source technologies that quantify the range of ideas tolerated in online political discourse. It measures public opinion by scraping Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, YouTube, Wikipedia and other platforms.
